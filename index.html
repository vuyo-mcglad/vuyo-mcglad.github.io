About Me.

I'm a creative professional with a deep passion for narrative and visual communication. With a background in art direction and design, I've crafted visuals that resonate with diverse audiences across various platforms. Since completing my education, I've contributed to several successful campaigns and initiatives, both as a designer and as a social media manager, where I developed a keen ability to merge creativity with strategic thinking.

Beyond my design work, I actively host events through platforms I founded like NarowBi and Kids Love Jazz, continuing to explore the intersection of culture, music, and storytelling. I'm excited to bring fresh ideas and unique perspectives to the industry and become a valuable asset to any creative team.

Tshepo Jeans

AboTshepo Mara

Tshepo Jeans are affordable and long-lasting custom made jeans. They can with stand the test of time by honouring the past, appreciating the present, and planning for the future. The concept "AboTshepo mara" is in conjunction with the jeans being durable. This concept glorifies women by utilising the stigma of aboTshepo as horrible guys in society who do not last in relationships. It is narrated by a woman. The tone of the advertisement will be light hearted and passionate. The essence of Tshepo Jeans' story and mission will be captured in this concept. Project Link.
